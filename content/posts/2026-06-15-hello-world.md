## Why this exists

I read a lot. Papers, blog posts, documentation, random Wikipedia rabbit holes at 2am. The problem is I forget most of it within a week.

Writing forces you to actually understand something — not just skim it. So this blog is partly a public notebook, partly a way to hold myself accountable to actually learning things deeply.

## What you'll find here

- Things I read and found interesting, explained in my own words
- AI experiments I'm running
- Projects I'm building to learn something new
- Occasional opinions on software engineering

## The stack

This site is built with **Python + FastAPI**, hosted on **Railway**, with blog posts written in Markdown. Simple, owned by me, no algorithmic feed.

If something here is useful to you, great. If not, it was useful to me writing it — which was the point.
